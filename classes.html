Private Sugarsweet Zoom Classes

How about a private zoom session for a Baking 101? Email us at fakeemail@sugarsweet.com for scheduling and pricing

For people signing up for private sessions we will send a kit with all the equipments and ingredients you would need a day prior to our class.

In light of the ongoing pandemic, we have cancelled our in-person classes. For more information, please reach out to our events team. Thank you for your patience and understanding during these difficult times.

About the class: Each class is 2 hours long. In each class, you will learn a variety of baking and decorating techniques from our professional bakers, which would include:

  • Basic cake decorations
  • Inscriptions
  • Pastry bag techniques
  • Flower cupcakes
  • Swirly cupcake
  • Piped cupcakes

At the end of the class, you will have your beautiful creations (a cake or six cupcakes, depending on the class) to enjoy
